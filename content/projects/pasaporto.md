## Context

an interactive way to explore passport mobility and visa permissions without reading a giant table.

## Problem

travel-permission datasets are useful but difficult to understand when relationships are presented only as rows and columns.

## What I built

- interactive 3D globe
- country selection and highlighting
- visa-permission data processing
- rendered country relationships
- Three.js and WebGL interaction

## Technical notes

the project focused on making geographic relationships easier to explore while keeping the data and rendering logic separated.

## What I learned

a visual interface can make a dataset feel intuitive, but only when interaction, camera movement, labels, and performance are kept under control.

## Next step

improve mobile controls, accessibility fallbacks, and the explanation of each visa category.
